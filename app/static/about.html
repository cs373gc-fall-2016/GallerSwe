About Artsnob

Anyone can be a snob if they know what they're talking about. ArtSnob is a website project for Glenn Downing's CS 373 Software Engineering course. It is the product of the group GallerSwe, comprised of Courtney Bohrer, Alex Ikeda, Rachel Andrews, Cullen Bounds, Sam Fernandez and Christopher Komplin. ArtSnob is an education tool meant to create a slick, easy and informational platform for art learning and discovery. Explore artists you've just found or see tons of examples of styles you want to become familiar with. The options are all there.
